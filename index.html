Account Management
Please note that the Rabt Network team cannot reset passwords or reactivate matrix.org accounts. If you have an email address attached to your account, you can reset your password through the app's login screen by selecting "Set a new password" and following the confirmation link sent to your email.
Using an email address when creating a Rabt Network account is optional; it makes it easier for others to discover you and helps you reset your password if needed. It's ultimately up to the administrator of the server. For accounts on the matrix.org homeserver, supplying an email address is optional, but may occasionally be required for abuse mitigation.
Your username is used to create a Matrix ID that uniquely identifies you — distinguishing you from others with the same display name — while keeping your email confidential.
Phone number registration is no longer supported on Rabt Network. Please register using a username and optionally add an email address to your account.

Settings
Click the dropdown menu under your name in the top-left corner of the web or desktop app and select Settings. From there you can change all account and general Rabt Network preferences.
Go to Settings → Notifications. You can customise notifications globally or per-room. The per-room options are:
  • Mute: No notifications, even for mentions or keywords.
  • Mentions only: Only your name and keywords trigger noisy alerts.
  • All messages: Silent notification for every message; noisy for mentions.
  • All messages (noisy): Every message triggers sound and visual alerts.
In Settings → Notifications, enable the toggle labelled "Enable email notifications". You'll then receive email alerts when you miss messages or receive new invites.
Navigate to Settings → General (accessible from the dropdown menu under your name in the top-left corner) and update your display name there.
We strongly recommend adding an email so you can recover your account if you lose your password. It also makes you easier to discover and is simpler to remember than a Matrix ID. You can add an email at any time in Settings → General — it's not required only at registration.

It depends on the history visibility setting:
  • Members (full history): New members can read all past messages.
  • Members since invited: New members only see messages sent after their invite.
  • Anyone: Non-members can "peek" at the room before joining (non-encrypted rooms only).
Note: changing history visibility doesn't retroactively affect already-sent messages.

Privacy, Abuse & Notices
Rabt Network is a client that lets you connect to any homeserver in the Matrix network — similar to how a browser lets you visit any website. Each homeserver independently manages its own privacy and abuse policies. For details on how your data is handled, please review the applicable privacy policy for your homeserver. If you have further questions, contact your homeserver's data protection officer.
