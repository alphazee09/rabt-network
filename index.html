Account Management
Please note that the Rabt Network team cannot reset passwords or reactivate matrix.org accounts. If you have an email address attached to your account, you can reset your password through the app's login screen by selecting "Set a new password" and following the confirmation link sent to your email.
Using an email address when creating a Rabt Network account is optional; it makes it easier for others to discover you and helps you reset your password if needed. It's ultimately up to the administrator of the server.
Your username is used to create a Matrix ID that uniquely identifies you — distinguishing you from others with the same display name — while keeping your email confidential.
Phone number registration is no longer supported on Rabt Network. Please register using a username and optionally add an email address to your account.

Settings
Click the dropdown menu under your name in the top-left corner and select Settings.
Go to Settings → Notifications. Per-room options:
  • Mute: No notifications at all.
  • Mentions only: Name/keywords trigger alerts.
  • All messages: Silent for every message, noisy for mentions.
  • All messages (noisy): Every message triggers sound & visual alerts.
In Settings → Notifications, enable the toggle "Enable email notifications".
Navigate to Settings → General and update your display name there.
Adding an email lets you recover your account if you lose your password and makes you easier to discover. Add one at any time in Settings → General.

Privacy, Abuse & Notices
Rabt Network is a client that lets you connect to any homeserver in the Matrix network. Each homeserver manages its own privacy and abuse policies independently. Please review the privacy policy for your homeserver for full details.
